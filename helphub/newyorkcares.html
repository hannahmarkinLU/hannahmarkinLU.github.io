We provide year-round volunteer opportunities for New Yorkers like you. Projects take place across the city in the following areas:

  • Educating children, teens, and adults
  • Serving those impacted by hunger and homelessness
  • Health and wellness for seniors and other vulnerable groups
  • Caring for public parks, community gardens, and green spaces

Available Volunteer Opportunities

Distribute Food to the Community

Hand out bags of healthy food and fresh produce to customers on the sidewalk of 86th street. This is a customer facing role that takes place outside rain or shine. Volunteers should feel comfortable working on their feet and lift up to 15 pounds.

Support Jobseekers

Prepare and perfect resumes with jobseekers at an agency dedicated to providing immediate, comprehensive and effective employment services for men and women returning from incarceration.

Learn and Practice SEL with Students

Engage students in their journey to becoming more productive, self-aware, and socially-aware individuals in and out of the classroom. Work with 3rd grade students in small groups to increase their competence in core social-emotional learning skills such as self-awareness, self-management, social awareness, relationship skills, and responsible decision making through a SEL curriculum and activities.
